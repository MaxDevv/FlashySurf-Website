How FlashySurf Works

FlashySurf transforms your regular browsing into productive learning time through three simple steps, working with any subject you want to master.

1

Browse Normally

Use the internet as you always do - read articles, watch videos, shop online. FlashySurf runs quietly in the background.

2

Answer Questions

Occasionally, a flashcard appears with a question from your selected collections. Take a moment to answer it.

3

Learn & Improve

Get instant feedback with explanations. Wrong answers trigger note-taking to reinforce learning. Then continue browsing!

It's That Simple

No dedicated study sessions required. No interrupting your workflow. Just passive learning that builds up over time, exactly like how you learned your native language.
